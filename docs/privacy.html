VidPush

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how VidPush (“the App”) handles information when you use it to connect YouTube/TikTok accounts and upload videos.

1. What VidPush does

VidPush is local software that uses OAuth to access your YouTube and TikTok accounts on your behalf so you can upload videos you choose. By default, it stores OAuth tokens on your computer in a local database file.

2. Information we process

  • OAuth tokens from Google/YouTube and TikTok, stored locally to keep you signed in.
  • Video files you select, temporarily processed for upload to the platforms you enable.
  • Basic metadata you provide (such as title) sent to the platforms you select.

3. What we do not do (default local setup)

  • We do not operate a central VidPush server that collects your videos or tokens by default.
  • We do not sell your personal information.

4. Third-party services

When you connect accounts or upload, Google/YouTube and TikTok receive data according to their own privacy policies. Review those policies on their websites.

5. Data retention

OAuth tokens remain on your device until you disconnect accounts or delete local App data. Temporary upload files are removed after upload jobs complete.

6. Security

You are responsible for protecting your computer, `.env` secrets, and OAuth credentials. Do not share your client secrets or commit them to public repositories.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date will reflect changes. If you deploy VidPush as a hosted service in the future, this policy should be updated to describe that deployment.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact the VidPush project maintainer via the contact method on the project repository.
